### System Requirements

To run a Zulip server, you will need:

- A dedicated machine or VM
- A supported OS: 
  - Ubuntu 20.04 Focal
  - Ubuntu 18.04 Bionic
  - Debian 10 Buster
- At least 2GB RAM, and 10GB disk space 
  - If you expect 100+ users: 4GB RAM, and 2 CPUs
- A hostname in DNS
- Credentials for sending email
