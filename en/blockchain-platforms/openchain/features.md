#### **Features**

Openchain offers the following key features:

*   Open Source
*   Instant Confirmation of Transactions
*   No Mining Fee
*   Multiple Levels of Control
*   Secure With Digital Signatures
*   Extensible
*   Scalable
*   Provision To Define Rules
*   Robust
*   Auditability of Transactions
