### **Features**

Following are the key features of phpList:

- Beautifully crafted.
- Translated.
- JSON API.
- Scheduled maintenance.
- Internationalization.
- Metrics.
- Opensource.
- Two-factor authentication.
- Manage users.
- Manage components &amp; groups.
- Manage Incidents.
- Subscribers for Notifications.
- Customize header, footer &amp; styles.
