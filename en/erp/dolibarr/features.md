### Features

Dolibarr has an excellent feature list and some of them are:

- Content Management System (CMS)
- E-Commerce
- Point Of Sale (POS)
- Opportunities
- Proposals
- Employees Management
- Product and Stock Management
- Finance and Billing
- Marketing
- Project Management
- API
- Integration with external apps
- Open Source
