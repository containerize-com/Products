### **Features**

Jitsi Meet offers the following key features:

- Open Source
- Self-Hosted
- Secure
- WebRTC Compatible
- Scalable
- Flexible
- Encryption
- Multi-Party Video Conference
- Support For Etherpad
- Invite Users
- Simulcasting
- Video/Audio Recording
- Extensible
