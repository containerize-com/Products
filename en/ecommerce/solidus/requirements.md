System Requirements
-------------------

Solidus is fully customizable eCommerce platform, built on the shared knowledge of brands.

- Rails 5.0.0 or newer
- Ruby 2.2.2 or newer
- SQLite 3
- ImageMagick
- bundler
- Linux 64-bit distributions supporting APT
