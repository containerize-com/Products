### **FAQs**

### What is Posthog?
In fact, Posthog is an open source data analytics solution that facilitates website owners in terms of event automation, data insights and cvonversion/retention tracking. 
### Does Posthog work for mobile and web?
Yes, it works for mobile and web both.
### How popular is the Posthog?
This business intelligence and analytics software is very popular with more than 3k stars on Github.
### Can I self-host Posthog?
Yes, you can host it on your own infrastructure. 
### Is Posthog supported properly?
This web analytics software has very active development community.
